About Us

Our CCA is a platform for students passionate about engineering, technology, and innovation. Members gain hands-on experience through competitions, projects, and teamwork.

We foster a collaborative environment where students can explore robotics, drones, and electronics while developing problem-solving skills and creative thinking.

VEX Robotics

Our VEX Robotics team competes in local and national tournaments, designing and building robots to complete specific challenges.

Design

Build and design competitive robots from scratch using CAD software and precision engineering principles.

Programming

Code autonomous and driver-controlled routines using VEXcode/ PROS, developing algorithms for optimal performance.

Competition

Participate in national VEX tournaments, testing skills against teams from across the nation.

Electronics

Learn circuit design, soldering, ESP, Arduino and microcontrollers while working on real-world electronics projects.

Members build everything from simple circuits to complex embedded systems, gaining practical skills in electronics engineering.

Circuit Design

Design and prototype electronic circuits using simulation software and breadboarding techniques.

Electronics Programming

Program microcontrollers and embedded systems using C++ and Arduino IDE for various applications.

Microcontrollers

Program Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and ESP32 boards to create interactive electronic projects.

Drones

Explore flight mechanics, drone assembly, and aerial programming through hands-on drone projects. Learn about aerodynamics, flight controllers, and coding.

Drone Assembly

Build custom drones from scratch, learning about frames, motors, ESCs, and flight controllers.

Flight Programming

Program a drone's flight behavior and control systems using specialized drone programming software.

Piloting Drones

Master drone piloting skills, flight maneuvers, and safety protocols for aerial operations.

Leadership

Develop leadership, communication, and project management skills by leading teams and mentoring juniors. Our leadership program prepares students for roles as team captains, project managers, and mentors.

What We Look For

We welcome students with diverse skills and interests. While prior experience is prioritised, we also look for enthusiasm and willingness to learn.

Passion

Curiosity and interest in technology and robotics. A drive to create and innovate.

Commitment

Willingness to learn, attend sessions regularly, and contribute to team projects.

Teamwork

Ability to collaborate, communicate effectively, and support fellow team members.
